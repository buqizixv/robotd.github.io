Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Robot D is a static website. We do not operate a server, database, or user accounts system. We collect no personal information directly. However, third-party services integrated into this site may collect information as described below.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Robot D uses your browser's localStorage to remember two preferences:

  • Language preference — whether you prefer English or Chinese (中文) content. Stored as a single key-value pair (lang). Not transmitted to any server.
  • Cookie consent acknowledgment — whether you have accepted the cookie notice. Stored as a single key-value pair (cookie-consent). Not transmitted to any server.

This data never leaves your browser. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

Google may use information about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. For more information about how Google uses data, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google Fonts

This website loads the Inter font family from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these font files, Google may receive technical information including your IP address. This is a standard part of how web fonts work. See Google Fonts Privacy FAQ for details.

3.3 GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information including IP addresses as part of serving web content. See GitHub Pages documentation for details.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. This site uses cookies in the following ways:

  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized ads and measure ad performance. These cookies may track your browsing across multiple websites.
  • Functional storage (localStorage) — As described in Section 2, we use localStorage to remember your language and consent preferences. These are not cookies but serve a similar function.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling advertising cookies will not remove ads from the site, but the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

5. GDPR Rights (European Union / EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access — You can request information about what personal data is processed.
  • Right to rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure — You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to object — You can object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing (including personalized ads).
  • Right to withdraw consent — You can withdraw your consent to cookie usage at any time by clearing your browser data for this site.

Since Robot D does not collect personal data directly, most of these rights pertain to data held by third-party services. For Google-specific data requests, visit Google Account.

6. CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion of personal information, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. Robot D does not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights regarding data held by third-party services, please refer to those services' privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Robot D is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

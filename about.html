About Robot D

Established 2026

Our Mission

Robot D tracks the most important developments in robotics and artificial intelligence — from humanoid robots entering factories to AI foundation models that can control any machine. We believe the robotics revolution is the most important technology story of this decade, and we're here to cover it with depth, accuracy, and clarity.

What We Cover

Our editorial scope spans six core areas:

  • Humanoid Robots — Figure AI, Tesla Optimus, Boston Dynamics Atlas, and the race to build general-purpose humanoid workers
  • Industrial Automation — Collaborative robots, AI-powered warehouse systems, and the transformation of manufacturing
  • AI & Robotics — Foundation models for robot control, reinforcement learning at scale, and the software that powers autonomous machines
  • Drones & UAVs — Delivery drones, autonomous inspection, and the regulatory frameworks enabling commercial drone operations
  • Medical Robotics — Surgical robots, micro-robots for drug delivery, and rehabilitation robotics
  • Research & Breakthroughs — Academic and industry research pushing the boundaries of what robots can do

Who We Are

Robot D is independently operated by a team of technology journalists and robotics engineers who believe that quality information about emerging technology should be freely accessible. Our content is written by humans, for humans — we cover robotics because we're genuinely excited about the field, not because an algorithm told us to.

Bilingual Coverage

Robot D publishes all content in both English and Chinese (中文). Our English coverage targets a global audience of robotics professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts. Our Chinese coverage serves the rapidly growing robotics community in China, which is home to some of the world's most innovative robotics companies and research institutions. Use the language toggle in the navigation bar to switch between languages at any time.

Technology

Robot D is a static website built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools, no server-side rendering — just clean, fast-loading pages optimized for readers and search engines alike. We deploy via GitHub Pages.
